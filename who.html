From Rhymes to Rhythms

Since childhood, I've been vibing with music. As soon as I cracked the code of reading and writing, I started spitting poetry like it was my daily mantra. School contests? You bet I owned the dance floor and belted out tunes like a mini rockstar, channeling my inner Beyoncé and Nicki Minaj. Dutch icons like B Brave and Aliyah fueled my playlist. High school was my rap revolution; I dropped some fierce freestyles. Dreaming of making my beats, I was scared to fail until a Beyoncé concert flipped the script. Now, six months later, I'm cooking up tracks, and my songwriting game is untouchable!

To game or not to game

Gaming has been my jam since the playground days. Back then, my brother and I rocked the PS3, conquering Minecraft, unleashing chaos in Call of Duty, and diving deep into narrative wonders like Detroit: Become Human. Then, my world shifted when I scored my first computer and unleashed Sims 4. Obsession? More like a full-blown addiction. I can't imagine a day without checking in on my virtual squad. Sims 4 is the culprit behind my career choice—I'm diving into the gaming world to unravel its secrets, decode mechanisms, and who knows, maybe drop some mind-blowing mods in the future!

Money Talks, I Translate

In the wise words of our honorary queen, Beyoncé Knowles, "If you ain't getting money, then you ain't got nothing for me." Truer words were never spoken! I'm all about that money-making hustle. Yet, let's be real, spending it is the ultimate thrill. Shoes, games, and vintage bags — my top three guilty pleasures. Because as much as the grind matters, so does treating yourself to the finest things in life. Beyoncé knows it, and so do I.
